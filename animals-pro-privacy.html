Privacy Policy for Animals Pro

Last updated: November 8th, 2023

Welcome to Animals Pro! I, as the developer, am committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines my data handling practices while using our mobile application. By using Animals Pro, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

I do not collect any personal information from our users. The app is designed for users of all ages and does not require an account or any personal information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

No Personal Data: Animals Pro does not collect or store any personal data from users. I do not gather information that can identify you as an individual.

3. App Sections and Features

  • Learning Section: In this section, users can learn the names of 64 different animals in various languages. The app may also pronounce the names of animals in the selected language.
  • Quiz Section: The quiz section allows users to test their knowledge by matching animal names with images. Quiz statistics, including user performance and progress, are stored locally on the user's device only. It's important to note that if the app is removed from the user's device, this data, including quiz stats and puzzle progression, will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. No personal information is collected during the quiz section.
  • Puzzle Section: In this section, users can complete animal puzzles of varying difficulties. Puzzle progression data is stored locally on the user's device. If the app is uninstalled, all puzzle progression data will be lost.
  • Settings Section: The settings section includes options to customize the app's appearance, language, and sound settings. It also provides links to our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions. Users can contact us via email and explore other apps developed by the same developer.

4. External Links

The app may contain links to external websites for the Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and Contact Information. These links will navigate users to my website or contact email.

Additionally, the other promoted apps are deeplinks that will forward the user to the respective app store page based on their device:

  • If the user opening the link is on an Android device, the link will direct them to the Google Play Store app page for the promoted app.
  • If the user opening the link is on an iOS device, the link will direct them to the Apple App Store app page for the promoted app.
  • For users on any other type of device, the link will redirect them to my website's app landing page.
